    One day, a monkey and a turtle
  were hungry and saw a banana
  tree floating in the river.
 
    They decided
  to divide the
  banana tree
  into two and
  plant it.
 
    The monkey kept teasing
  the turtle that his tree
  would not grow.
 
    They went to visit the tree
  that they had planted.
   
  The monkey was shocked
  and jealous because the
  turtle's tree grew into a
  banana tree while his
  tree stayed the same.
 
    The monkey climbed
  the turtle's tree and
  ate some bananas.
  The turtle was angry
  and yelled at the
  monkey.
 
    Since the turtle cannot climb
  the tree, he placed thorns
  around the tree to teach
  the monkey a lesson.
 
    Monkey fell down the tree
  and was hurt by the thorns.
  He decided to take revenge
  on the turtle.
 
    The foolish monkey did exactly
  that; he picked the turtle up
  and threw it into the river.
 
    The turtle gladly came up to the
  surface of the water and
  mocked the monkey.
 
    As for the banana tree? Well, the weeds
  took over the tree, for the monkey was
  too foolish to know how to take care of it.
  Since that day, monkeys and turtles
  have been bitter enemies.
 
    MORAL OF
  THE STORY

Do not be greedy, always
show kindness.
