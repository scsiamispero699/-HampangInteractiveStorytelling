Who we are

We are students from Mapua Malayan Colleges Mindanao
majoring in Bachelor of Multimedia Arts that conducted a thesis
study entitled: HAMPANG: An Interactive Storytelling for Students
with Dyslexia. This study aims to help create interactive storytelling
as an alternative reading material for students with Dyslexia.
