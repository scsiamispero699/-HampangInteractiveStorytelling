    Once there was a deer who is very
  proud of himself. While walking, he
  saw a snail moving slowly.
 
    The deer challenged
  the snail into a race.
 
    The snail went
  home and told
  the other snails
  about the race.
  They made a plan
  to outsmart
  the deer.
 
    When Sunday came, they
  took up hiding places a little
  distant from each other. All
  the way from the starting
  point to the finish line.
 
    Then the race started.
 
    During the race, the deer tried
  to increase his pace, but the
  snail is still ahead of him. His
  sweat spilled, his legs felt weak
  and his breathing panted.
 
    The deer kept running, until he
  finally saw the finish line. He
  felt happy, because he thought
  that he won the race.
 
    How surprised the deer was
  because he saw the snail sitting on top
  of the rock near the finish line.
 
    He approached the snail and bowed
  his head in recognition of defeat.
 
     MORAL OF
  THE STORY

Always try to be the best, but never
underestimate other's abilities.
