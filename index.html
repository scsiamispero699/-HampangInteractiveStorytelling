Hello!

Interactive storytelling that is perfect for kids from 6-14 years old.
Practice your child's vocabulary and enjoy interactive storytelling
that will bring them to the fantasies of Filipino stories! Explore
three Filipino stories and learn while dragging and clicking on
different games! So let's go. HAMPANG now!

... HAMPANG- a Hiligaynon term for the word "play" because we want
kids to have a unique experience while learning in a gamified way.
Featured here are three Filipino short stories that will help your
child learn life lessons that they could bring into their everyday
success. Kids can also enjoy two different games that will make
learning easier by just clicking and dragging. Dyslexic-friendly
interactive storytelling that will show the wonders
of Filipino stories.
